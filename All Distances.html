MJ Travel NorthWest is your private chauffeur service based in UK, specializing in van transfers for groups of 1 to 20 passengers. Whether for business trips, family events, or leisure travel, we offer a high-end, comfortable transportation service. Our modern vehicles are equipped with the latest technology and offer optimal comfort to ensure your journey is as pleasant as possible. All our drivers are experienced professionals, trained to ensure your journey is safe and cared for. We’re committed to offering fixed prices, announced in advance, with no surprises.
